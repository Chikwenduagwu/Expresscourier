About SwiftEx

We started as a small team with one mission — make shipping simple, transparent, and reliable for everyone.

Our Story

Built on the Belief That Logistics Should Just Work

SwiftEx was founded in 2016 by a group of logistics professionals who were frustrated by an industry that treated customers as an afterthought. Parcels got lost. Tracking pages said nothing useful. Customer service was unreachable. The technology was decades behind what people expected in every other part of their lives.

We built SwiftEx from scratch to fix these problems — not incrementally, but fundamentally. We invested in real-time tracking infrastructure before it was industry standard. We built our customer support tools in-house so agents could actually see what customers saw. We made pricing transparent and calculable upfront rather than full of surprises at checkout.

Today we operate in 180+ countries, deliver over 2 million parcels a year, and maintain a 99.8% on-time delivery rate — but we still think the best version of what we can be is ahead of us, not behind.

Sustainability

Our Commitment to the Planet

Logistics has a meaningful environmental footprint and we take that responsibility seriously. In 2024 we published our first full carbon report, committed to net-zero operations by 2035, and began transitioning 40% of our last-mile fleet to electric vehicles.

Every shipment made through SwiftEx now comes with a carbon footprint estimate. Business customers can access a full sustainability dashboard showing their total emissions and options to offset at checkout. We've partnered with certified reforestation programmes in Ghana, Indonesia, and Brazil to offset the emissions we haven't yet eliminated.

We don't think sustainability should cost more. Most of our green initiatives are funded by efficiency gains — smarter routing, better vehicle utilisation, and reduced packaging waste. When you ship with SwiftEx, you're choosing a company that's actively working to make the logistics industry better for the planet.
