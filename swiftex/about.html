Our Story

Built on the Belief That Logistics Should Just Work

Express Courier was founded by a group of logistics professionals frustrated by an industry that treated customers as an afterthought. Parcels got lost. Tracking pages said nothing useful. Customer service was unreachable. The technology was decades behind what people expected in every other part of their lives.

We built Express Courier from scratch to fix these problems . not incrementally, but fundamentally. We invested in real-time tracking infrastructure before it was industry standard. We built our customer support tools in-house so agents could actually see what customers saw. We made pricing transparent and calculable upfront rather than full of surprises at checkout.

Today we operate in 180+ countries, deliver over 2 million parcels a year, and maintain a 99.8% on-time delivery rate . but we still think the best version of what we can be is ahead of us, not behind.

Our Values

What We Stand For

  • Radical Transparency

    We show you exactly where your parcel is, what it cost, and why . no fine print, no hidden fees, no vague status messages.

  • Speed Without Compromise

    Fast delivery matters, but not at the cost of care. Every parcel is handled with the same attention whether it's a letter or a fragile antique.

  • Global Reach, Local Feel

    We operate worldwide but stay close to communities. Our local partners know their regions and are empowered to deliver brilliant service.

  • Sustainability

    We measure and publish our carbon footprint. We're actively transitioning our fleet to low-emission vehicles and offsetting what we can't yet eliminate.

  • Genuine Support

    When something goes wrong, we own it. Our team is empowered to resolve issues without endless escalation chains or scripted non-answers.

Sustainability

Our Commitment to the Planet

Logistics has a meaningful environmental footprint . and we take that responsibility seriously. In 2024 we published our first full carbon report, committed to net-zero operations by 2035, and began transitioning 40% of our last-mile fleet to electric vehicles.

Carbon Reporting

Every shipment comes with a carbon footprint estimate. Full transparency on our environmental impact.

Electric Fleet

40% of our last-mile delivery fleet is transitioning to electric vehicles by end of 2025.

Reforestation

Partnered with certified programmes in Ghana, Indonesia, and Brazil to offset remaining emissions.

Net Zero by 2035

Smarter routing, better vehicle utilisation, and reduced packaging waste fund our green initiatives.
