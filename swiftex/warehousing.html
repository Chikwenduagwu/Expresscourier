Express Courier's warehousing and fulfilment network provides businesses of all sizes with scalable, strategically located storage and distribution infrastructure. Whether you need short-term overflow storage, a fully outsourced e-commerce fulfilment operation, or a complex multi-temperature distribution hub, our facilities and teams are equipped to deliver.

1. Service Overview

Our warehousing division operates over 2.4 million square feet of storage space across 14 strategically located facilities in the UK, Europe, and the Middle East. Each facility is directly connected to our transport network, enabling seamless inbound freight receipt and outbound distribution with minimal dwell time.

Services range from simple pallet storage with pick-and-pack, through to fully managed 3PL (third-party logistics) operations where our team acts as an extension of your own warehouse and fulfilment function.

2. Storage Solutions

We offer flexible storage configurations to suit every product type and volume requirement. Pricing is based on space used, with no long-term commitment required for short-term storage. Contract storage with guaranteed capacity allocation is available for businesses with predictable volumes.

Storage Types

  • Pallet racking: Standard euro-pallet and block-stacked storage; ideal for bulk goods
  • Shelving & bin storage: Optimised for small parcel and e-commerce product ranges
  • Mezzanine storage: Multi-level storage for high-SKU-count operations
  • Drive-in racking: High-density storage for homogeneous goods with lower picking frequency
  • Secure caged storage: For high-value, restricted, or age-verified products
  • Bulk floor storage: For irregular or oversized items unsuitable for racking

3. Order Fulfilment

Our fulfilment service handles the complete order-to-despatch cycle on your behalf. Inbound stock is received, checked, and put away; orders received from your e-commerce platform, ERP, or marketplace are automatically picked, packed, and despatched . typically within the same business day for orders placed before 15:00.

Fulfilment Process

  • Inbound receiving: Goods-in checking against purchase orders; discrepancy reporting within 24 hours
  • Putaway: Systematic location assignment using our WMS for optimal pick efficiency
  • Pick & pack: RF-scanner guided picking with accuracy verification at packing station
  • Packing: Standard Express Courier packaging or custom-branded materials (surcharge applies)
  • Despatch: Same-day despatch for orders received before 15:00 cut-off
  • Returns processing: Inspection, restocking, quarantine, or disposal per your policy

Platform integrations included. Our WMS connects directly to Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon Seller Central, eBay, Magento, and all major ERPs including SAP, NetSuite, and Microsoft Dynamics . at no additional integration fee for standard connectors.

5. Cold Chain & Specialist Storage

Several of our facilities include temperature-controlled storage zones certified for pharmaceutical, food, and cosmetic products. Our cold chain capability supports GDP-compliant pharmaceutical distribution and HACCP-certified food storage, with full audit trails and temperature logging.

  • Ambient (15–25°C): Controlled ambient zones for temperature-sensitive non-refrigerated goods
  • Chilled (2–8°C): GDP-compliant pharmaceutical cold storage and food-grade chilled
  • Frozen (−18°C and below): Frozen food and biological sample storage
  • Controlled temperature (CRT): 15–25°C with humidity control for pharmaceutical products
  • Temperature monitoring: Continuous electronic monitoring with out-of-range alerts and data logging

6. Bonded & Customs Warehousing

Our bonded warehouse facilities are HMRC-approved customs warehouses (Type C and Type D), allowing goods to be stored under customs control without payment of import duties until the goods are released for free circulation or re-exported. This is particularly valuable for businesses importing from outside the UK or EU who wish to defer duty liability.

  • Duty suspension: Import goods and defer duty and VAT until sale or release
  • Fiscal warehousing: VAT deferral on eligible goods stored under fiscal warehouse authorisation
  • Re-export: Goods can be re-exported from bond without payment of UK/EU import duties
  • Processing under customs control: Light manufacturing and processing permitted under authorisation
  • HMRC compliance: Full compliance management; we hold the warehouse authorisation on your behalf

All Express Courier warehouse operations are managed through our proprietary cloud-based WMS, which provides real-time inventory visibility, automated replenishment alerts, location management, and comprehensive reporting. Clients receive a secure portal login with access to their own inventory data, order status, and performance dashboards at all times.

  • Real-time inventory: Live stock levels by SKU, location, and batch/serial number
  • Order management: Live order status from receipt through to despatch confirmation
  • Reporting suite: Inventory ageing, throughput, accuracy, and cost reports on demand
  • API access: Pull live inventory data and push orders directly from your systems
  • Alerts & notifications: Low-stock, slow-moving, and expiry date alerts configurable per SKU

All Express Courier warehouse facilities operate to internationally recognised security and quality standards.
